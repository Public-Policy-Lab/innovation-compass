Compass Quiz

Get personalized recommendations backed by experts on how to chart your path forward.

This quiz creates your personal Innovation Compass—a quick guide to your strengths and areas for growth in innovation. Based on your answers and tips from successful founders, you'll get customized recommendations.

You can answer as yourself or from your team's perspective, depending on your business size.

About the Quiz

We worked with innovators and experts to identify 40 key practices for building a successful venture. These practices fall into three categories:

  • Community: Building the network needed to grow your venture.
  • Impact: Realizing your vision and proving your venture's purpose.
  • Entrepreneurship: Turning ideas into a strong, lasting business.

The quiz will ask about each practice to assess your preparedness.

How to Rate Yourself

Preparedness means being ready with a combination of knowledge, experience, and resources. For example, if you know what you need but don't have funding or tools yet, you're “somewhat prepared.”
